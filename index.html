How do I play?

Welcome to

The first player is chosen to start the game as the Buyer. Everyone else starts as a Supplier. The Suppliers get 4 qualifications each.

Starting to the left of the Employer, each Supplier “interviews” by explaining why their qualifications make them the best fit for the Job. Suppliers reveal their qualifications to the Buyer, one at a time, and describe how each one makes them qualified for the Job. Because these qualifications are chosen at random and not revealed to the player until they are chosen, the applicants have to think on their feet.

After all Suppliers have had a turn, the Buyer chooses which Applicant they feel is the most qualified and the Supplier gets a point. A new buyer is chosen and the cycle begins again.
